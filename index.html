How It Works

A clear path from inquiry to title.

Land titling has a defined legal sequence — here's how we move through it together.

1

Consultation & Verification

We review your property and documents, and verify the land's status and location.

2

Document Preparation

We gather and prepare all required paperwork, including BIR clearances where needed.

3

Filing & Registration

We file with the relevant offices — BIR, Registry of Deeds, and local assessor — on your behalf.

4

Title Release

We track the process through to completion and hand you your finalized, registered title.
